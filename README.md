# SAP_project
The Order-to-Cash (O2C) process is one of the most critical business processes in any 
organization. It encompasses all the steps from receiving a customer order to realizing the 
payment for goods or services delivered. In SAP, the O2C cycle is primarily handled through
the SAP Sales and Distribution (SAP SD) module, which is tightly integrated with other 
modules such as SAP MM (Materials Management) and SAP FI (Financial Accounting).
This project report presents a comprehensive step-by-step walkthrough of the Order-to-Cash
process as implemented in SAP SD. It covers the entire lifecycle of a sales transaction, from 
pre-sales activities to the final posting of incoming payments.
